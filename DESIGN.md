# Design System: Amber Earth & Electric Blue
 
## Vision and Narrative
This design system transition moves away from high-contrast neon aesthetics toward a warm, sophisticated "Amber Earth" palette. It is designed for professional tools that require a sense of reliability and groundedness, balanced with high-energy focal points. The dark mode context provides a canvas for rich, organic tones that reduce eye strain while maintaining a vibrant, modern feel.
 
## Color Palette
The palette is built on a foundation of warm neutrals and burnt oranges, accented by a sharp digital blue.
 
*   **Primary (#db7202):** A rich, burnt orange used for primary actions, buttons, and critical branding. It provides warmth and visibility against dark surfaces.
*   **Secondary (#a16b42):** A muted, clay-like brown that handles secondary actions, chips, and supporting UI elements, reinforcing the organic aesthetic.
*   **Tertiary (#009bf1):** A bright, electric blue used for functional highlights, progress indicators, or decorative badges to prevent the palette from feeling overly monochromatic.
*   **Neutral (#83746b):** A warm, taupe-based neutral used for backgrounds and surfaces, creating a softer alternative to pure black or cold grays.
 
## Typography
The typographic system emphasizes clarity and a contemporary geometric feel.
 
*   **Headlines:** Plus Jakarta Sans provides a modern, high-tech energy with its clean, open proportions.
*   **Body & Labels:** Inter ensures maximum readability across all screen sizes, maintaining a neutral but professional tone for functional text.
 
## Structure and Shape
The interface conveys a sense of precision and professional structure.
 
*   **Roundedness (Level 1):** Subtle corner radii provide a soft touch to an otherwise rigorous and structured layout. It avoids the playfulness of pill shapes in favor of a crisp, architectural look.
*   **Spacing (Level 2):** Standard spacing provides a balanced data density, ensuring that the interface feels organized and breathable without wasting screen real estate.
 
## Design Principles
1.  **Grounded Energy:** Use the orange tones to lead the user's eye, while the neutral taupe keeps the experience focused.
2.  **Digital Contrast:** Use the tertiary blue sparingly to break the warm color scheme and signal specific "active" or "live" states.
3.  **Refined Precision:** Maintain the subtle roundedness to ensure the UI feels modern but remains grounded in professional utility.
